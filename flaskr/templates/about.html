About

Hello, my name is Clement. I take photos of the untouched nature and notes of my adventures. I found myself in the middle of nowhere and learnt a lot out of my comfort zone, fighting the elements and crash testing my resiliency. I hitchhiked more than 40,000 km and spent hundreds days through deserts, roads, rainforests, villages, lifestyles. Photography is my exploration tool in a way to widen our horizon toward remote wilderness. I prove with photos that the most beautiful things are natural. But photos do not keep snow on tops, native forests and wildlife, crystal clear rivers, pure mountain lakes, etc. I am not the example to follow but I do not act to greenwash myself and feel responsible, I just act.
