QMapShack3D

Plan, edit, and share tracks on interactive and immersive maps.

Key Features:

  • Load online/offline maps,
  • Commercial-grade and government maps and data ready to use,
  • Highly configurable 2D map viewer and a real 3D world,
  • Support for tracks, routes, waypoints and areas,
  • Create, edit, save GPX files and other formats.
Basically, QMapShack3D is QMapShack plus 3D and maps.
